About the Project

Welcome to our analysis of Spotify trends! We explore the correlation between users' top 5 song preferences and external factors such as holidays. Using Spotify's API, we aim to uncover valuable insights that can benefit streaming platforms, marketers, and cultural analysts.

Project Goals

  • 🎶 Analyze Spotify data to identify correlations with external factors.
  • 📊 Use visualizations like heat maps and bar graphs to extract insights.
  • 🌍 Help streaming platforms enhance user engagement.
  • 📈 Provide valuable insights for marketing and advertising campaigns.
